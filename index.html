The realistic wave simulation uses Phil Crowther's Ocean3 & Ocean4 modules (WebGL2 iFFT and WebGPU compute-shader iFFT). Much of the inner WebGPU plumbing is Attila Schroeder's work. Amazing stuff — thank you both!
